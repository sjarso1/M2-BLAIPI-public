Building on shared science and open data

This work builds on the genetic subgroup framework for Burkitt lymphoma described by Thomas et al. (2023) and on prior independent study of malaria and endemic Burkitt lymphoma (Wichtner, 2024).

We acknowledge the public data resources and the broader research community whose datasets and methods make this work possible.
