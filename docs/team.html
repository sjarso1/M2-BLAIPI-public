Team

Lab and Institution

Jarso Lab — Johns Hopkins University, Engineering for Professionals (Applied Biomedical Engineering).

Project Lead

Principal Investigator and Research Mentor: Samson Jarso, PhD, Johns Hopkins University.

Student and Collaborator Roles

The project is carried out by a biomedical-engineering student lead (biological interpretation, feature engineering, and modeling) working with a computer-science student lead (data harmonization and analysis pipeline), under faculty mentorship.

Acknowledgments

This work builds on the genetic subgroup framework for Burkitt lymphoma described by Thomas et al. (2023) and on prior independent study of malaria and endemic Burkitt lymphoma (Wichtner, 2024). We acknowledge the public data resources and the broader research community whose datasets and methods make this work possible.
